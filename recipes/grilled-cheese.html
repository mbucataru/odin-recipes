Grilled Cheese

Mouthwatering, delicious grilled cheese sandwiches

The grilled cheese sandwich is the embodiment of American cuisine. Easy to make, delicious, and easy to share with your family.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • American Cheese
  • Mayonaise
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Take two slices of bread, add mayo to the outside of each slice, and add butter to the inside of each.
  2. Place 1-2 slices of American cheese between the buttered sides of the bread.
  3. Preheat a non-stick pan over medium heat. After pan is hot, place sandwich down with the mayo side on the skillet.
  4. When the sandwich is golden brown and the cheese begins to melt, flip sandwich.
  5. Once both sides are golden brown and melted, remove from pan, let it cool slightly, and enjoy!
